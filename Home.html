Welcome to AquaShwe


We are here to help you with multiple designs of the kitchen, bath and living spaces, limited only by imagination and brought to fruition with the finest handcrafted stainless steel cabinetry in the world.


Our qualified and experienced team of engineers, architects and designers get together to create home designs which suit all palettes and preferences. Our aim has always been strong commitment towards customer satisfaction, and intricate detailing in all our projects.


With AquaShwe, you get to choose from a vast selection of home plans and designs with expert consultation. We are also open for customization in our existing designs at the most affordable prices. Our state-of-the-art 3D visualization tools, enable you to see exactly how your dream house will look even before it is built.


We also offer a wide range of interior design services, that truly help your imagination come to life. From choosing the perfect colour palette to selecting the right furniture and accessories, our team focuses on building a space that is both efficient and jaw-dropping.
